Because [Blaak Selectie](http://www.blaakselectie.nl/) existed for 3 years, Mark and Sandra wanted to organize an event for (.NET) Developers and asked me to help. To let the audience decide which presentation would be delivered, we asked them to choose between ‘Messaging’, ‘(Distributed) Transactions’ and ‘Distributed Systems Design’. The last one won and was presented on February 27th in Rotterdam in front of a great audience of 70 people. I had a blast and according to many reactions both personally and on Twitter and Facebook, the evening was a succes for both the audience and me.
