[Jan Tielens released RC1](http://weblogs.asp.net/jan/archive/2004/06/02/146932.aspx) of his workflow engine for SharePoint. Workflow was a feature that was in older versions of SharePoint, but in the latest, Microsoft removed it. The short period of time I was working with it, it was definitly one thing I missed.

Jan has a [video](http://blogs.leadit.be/files/ApprovalRouteSample.wmv) on what it can do and you can download it from [GotDotNet](http://www.gotdotnet.com/community/workspaces/workspace.aspx?ID=C07F64E8-8229-49A1-B160-B24C89122894).
