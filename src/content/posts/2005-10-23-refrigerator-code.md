During [MIND](https://bloggingabout.net/2005/10/12/9695), a colleague asked the audience if they were developing sexy shit. Bob Koss says it more politically correct, and calls it [refrigerator code](http://www.butunclebob.com/ArticleS.BobKoss.RefrigeratorCode).

While doing a TDD class, he explains the rules of [Simple Design](http://xp.c2.com/XpSimplicityRules.html). A student called it refrigerator code, which is code you’re so proud of you want to take home and hang it on the refrigerator, right alongside of your children’s drawings.
