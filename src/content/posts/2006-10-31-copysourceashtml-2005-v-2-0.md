In the past, I used [Colin Coller](http://www.jtleigh.com/people/colin/blog/)‘s CopySourceAsHtml in Visual Studio.NET 2003.Once Visual Studio 2005 was released, [Derick Baily](http://www.avocadosoftware.com/csblogs/dredge/default.aspx) and [Jason Haley](http://jasonhaley.com/blog/default.aspx) released a modified version and installer for it. I downloaded it and [used it since](https://bloggingabout.net/2006/02/10/11019). But it seems Colin has updated his version [since june 11th](http://www.jtleigh.com/people/colin/blog/archives/2006/06/copysourceashtm_9.html).

> I’ve just uploaded the first official release of [CopySourceAsHtml](http://www.jtleigh.com/people/colin/software/CopySourceAsHtml/) for Microsoft Visual Studio 2005.This release has a leaner, meaner, refactored codebase that fixes a few minor defects and takes advantage of new features in Visual Studio 2005 and .NET 2.0.This will be the codebase that future releases will be built from.

[Download it here](http://www.jtleigh.com/people/colin/software/CopySourceAsHtml/).
