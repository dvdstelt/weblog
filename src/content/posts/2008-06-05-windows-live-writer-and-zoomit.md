Two tools I absolutely love!
**Windows Live Writer   
**A new version was just released, June 2nd. [Download it here](http://windowslivewriter.spaces.live.com/blog/cns!D85741BB5E0BE8AA!1508.entry).
**Sysinternals ZoomIt   
**Version 2.0 was released on May 28.I just [downloaded](http://technet.microsoft.com/en-us/sysinternals/bb897434.aspx) that version myself and it has some nice features. However, I did not know about all 1.x existing features myself. I just saw a part of the TechEd keynote and noticed this guy was drawing rectangles and arrows. I thought : “What tool is this guy using? Can it be my favorite tool ZoomIt?” And yes he was!!!
* Draw a **rectangle** with the **control** key.
* Draw a **line** with the **shift** key.
* Draw an **arrow** with **control and shift** keys.

Now I did not know that! New features in 2.0 (afaik) are:
* Enable a clear **sketch pad** with the **W** (white) key or **K** (black) key.
* **Save** a drawing with **CTRL+S** * **Change pen width** by holding ctrl and zooming with the mouse wheel.

Sw33t!!!
