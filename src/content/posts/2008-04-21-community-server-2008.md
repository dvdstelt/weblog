Now that was a smooth transition. Within minutes BloggingAbout.NET was upgraded from version 2007.1 to Community Server 2008.We’ve gotten a brand new license from the great folks over at [Telligent](http://telligent.com/), the folks behind [Community Server](http://communityserver.com/).

For logged in users, the [frontpage](https://bloggingabout-linux.azurewebsites.net/) will show recent activity of blogs, posts, threads and friends you’re linked to or were active in. Not logged in users will see a welcome message and latest activities. That page is quite empty with no recent activity, I’m looking into it why this is. Also not all posts for every blog comes up in the [blogs overview](https://bloggingabout-linux.azurewebsites.net/blogs/) when you’re not logged in.

If you see any other problems or have questions, don’t hesitate to [contact us](https://bloggingabout-linux.azurewebsites.net/blogs/dennis/contact.aspx).
