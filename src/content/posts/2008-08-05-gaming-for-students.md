We used to have an XBox at Class-A for students to play on. But the older it got, the less students were interested in playing with it. So we decided to have a new console and opted for either Nintendo Wii or PlayStation 3.I took an investigation upon me to see which was most interesting. Wii has of course great controllers, but PlayStation 3 way better graphics and more important, cooler games to play with multiple people. Wii can’t even beat that, as it can’t even handle splitscreen from a performance point of view!

Anyway, today Astrid and I connected a 40” TV and the [PlayStation 3](http://nl.playstation.com/ps3/) together with [Guitar Hero 3](http://www.insidegamer.nl/playstation3/guitarheroiiilegendsofrock) and two guitars, [Buzz!: Quiz TV](http://www.insidegamer.nl/playstation3/buzzquiztv) with four buzzers and [Call of Duty 4](http://www.insidegamer.nl/playstation3/callofduty4modernwarfare), because of the 4 player splitscreen multiplayer part. [Motorstorm Pacific Rift](http://www.insidegamer.nl/playstation3/motorstormpacificrift/) and [LittleBigPlanet](http://www.insidegamer.nl/playstation3/littlebigplanet) (check out [this video](http://dennisvanderstelt.nl/blog/littlebigplanet/)) are already on pre-order and we’ll see about other games.

Here’s me testing out Guitar Hero 3.After seeing the images I think I look much too <strike>sweet</strike> soft to be a Rock Legend, but I did enjoy playing the game though.
