![Happy New Year Champagne](/images/happy-new-year/happynewyear.jpg)Happy new year everyone!!!

Of course we wish you all the best for this new year! At the ending of 2006 a lot of new technologies have been released that most of us aren’t working with yet. I’m thinking the .NET Framework 3.0, Office System and Vista among others. I hope we’ll all get to work with them this year as they’re making my life as developer a lot more fun!

Besides that, also the best to everyone in health and with your families!

At [Class-A](http://www.class-a.nl/) there’s happening a lot this year. First of all, [Pascal Naber](/blogs/pascal/) will join us this month. I hope he’ll start blogging a bit more here! We also have some cool new trainings to offer you, among others the Windows Communication Training that I’ll personally be giving. If you’re interested, be sure to [sign up for it](http://www.class-a.nl/index.aspx?id=12&catID=6&sid=14). 😉

Again, all the best for this new year!
