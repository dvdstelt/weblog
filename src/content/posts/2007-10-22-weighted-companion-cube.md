![I_want_one_by_berdwin](/images/weighted-companion-cube/i_want_one_by_berdwin_thumb.jpg)If you’ve finished [Portal](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Portal_%28video_game%29) by now, you know what I’m talking about. If not, go boot up Portal and play it until you’re finished!

Since I’ve played it, I’m in love with the companion cube. And as it seems, I’m not the only one! The best news is, Valve has recognized the love and promised we can buy our very own companion cube from the [valve store](http://store.valvesoftware.com/)! Read the initial [email to Gabe Newell and the reply](http://forums.penny-arcade.com/showthread.php?p=3195741&highlight=companion+cube+valve#post3195741) of the store manager (?) Jason Holtman who says they’ve been in production for two months and ready in time for the holidays. I sure hope so, because I’ve contacted the appropriate person at Class-A already to make sure it’s my birthday gift! 😉

In my dreams I already see my own companion cube, waiting for me on a shelve at Valve’s store!!! 😉
