[![Rotterdam skyline with rainbow](/images/rainbows-over-rotterdam/secondarythumb.aspx)](/photos/dennis_van_der_stelt/picture42113.aspx)Erwyn is regularly blogging about his Amsterdam apartment and skyline. Although I don’t actually live in Rotterdam, I’m currently working at one of the most beautifully locations available, the [<font color="#669966">Van Nelle fabriek</font>](null). It’s a ‘rijksmonument’, a very old building with governmental funding to keep it in its original state. A great location to work at.

Because it’s fall a lot of rain is falling, but from times to times the sun also shows itself. This resulted in a beautifully view from my workspace onto the skyline of Rotterdam!
