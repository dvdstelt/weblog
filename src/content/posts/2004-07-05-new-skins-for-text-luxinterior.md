Gosatango has created [a new skin](http://mixtaper.com/blogs/gosatango/articles/221.aspx) for .Text, called LuxInterior. It’s a really nice skin which I probably want myself! It also has the calender included, described by [two](http://scottonwriting.net/sowblog/posts/708.aspx) [guys](http://blogs.pingpoet.com/overflow/archive/2004/02/14/304.aspx) before. You might want to have a look at it. It comes in two flavors, light and dark. If you’re a blogger here on Wasabi, you can use it as soon as I’ve installed it. That will be the day, you can rip me away from my [Star Wars Galaxies](http://www.starwarsgalaxies.com/), that is! 🙂
