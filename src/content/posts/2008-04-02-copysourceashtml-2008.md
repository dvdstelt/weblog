**UPDATE :** CopySourceAsHtml can be [obtained from CodePlex](http://copysourceashtml.codeplex.com/).

I was looking for CopySourceAsHtml for Visual Studio 2008, but for some reason it’s not on the ‘official’ page so I had to search for it. There are multiple versions, but on Guy Burstein’s blog there’s more information and the only [RTM version](http://blogs.microsoft.co.il/blogs/bursteg/archive/2007/11/21/copy-source-as-html-copysourceashtml-for-visual-studio-2008-rtm.aspx) I could find.

I always search on [Jan Schreuder](/blogs/jschreuder/archive/2007/08/19/how-to-change-settings-in-copysourceashtml-for-use-with-community-server.aspx) his weblog for the following piece of css to include:

<div style="width: 100%;font-family: lucida console;background: #f5f5f5;color: black;font-size: 10pt;overflow: auto;border: #cccccc 1pt solid;padding: 1pt">

border-top: windowtext 1pt solid;

border-top-color: #CCCCCC;

padding-top: 1pt;

border-left: windowtext 1pt solid;

border-left-color: #CCCCCC;

padding-left: 1pt;

border-right: windowtext 1pt solid;

border-right-color: #CCCCCC;

padding-right: 1pt;

border-bottom: windowtext 1pt solid;

border-bottom-color: #CCCCCC;

padding-bottom: 1pt;

width: 100%;

overflow: auto;

background-color: #F5F5F5;      

</div>

Can’t live without CopySourceAsHtml now, can we?!
