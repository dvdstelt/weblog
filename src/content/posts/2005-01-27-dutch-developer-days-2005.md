Seems I had the schedule interpreted wrong, but here’s my current schedule for the event:
* [Pre conference day : PCT1 – Visual Studio Team System](http://www.microsoft.com/netherlands/msdn/devdays/sessies_PCT.aspx#PCT1)  
* Track 1 : Integrating the Enterprise  
* Track 2 : [Software Factories](http://www.microsoft.com/netherlands/msdn/devdays/sessies_D.aspx#D2)  
* Track 3 : [Enterprise Applications with EntLib](http://www.microsoft.com/netherlands/msdn/devdays/sessies_D.aspx#D3)  
* Track 4 : Web Services with ASMX 2.0 by [Alex Thissen](http://www.alexthissen.nl/weblog/PermaLink.aspx?Guid=fc608906-5d01-4bcc-8326-a770ad358275)

I was also thinking to go to TDD with TS for track 4, but I’d like to see Alex present this one!
