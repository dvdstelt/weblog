There are a few adjustments that are really nice, for example project grooming was added.

One quote from the guide

> The Scrum framework consists of Scrum Teams and their associated roles, events, artifacts, and rules. Each component within the framework serves a specific purpose and is essential to
> Scrum’s success and usage.

Text was added to emphasize that <span style="text-decoration: underline">Scrum is an all or nothing proposition</span>. You either do all the practices, or you’re not doing Scrum. I have to agree on it, after visiting a Scrum training by Jeff Sutherland. Before the training it was my believe that the Scrum framework was build with knowledge from the past and a lot of trial and error. Instead it was build and introduced at OOPSLA’95 through a lot of studies, whitepapers and other resources. During the two day course, Jeff Sutherland took little over half a day to emphasize on this. Also by providing lots of examples on how Scrum succeeded and how we should implement and use Scrum.

I’m a believer and want and will to advocate Scrum wherever I come and to whomever may ask. ;-)
