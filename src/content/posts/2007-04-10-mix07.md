![](/images/mix07/mix07logo5b85d.png) I’ll be attending [MIX’07](http://www.visitmix.com/) in Las Vegas this year as well as the parallel MDEC conference for Mobile development. One of my goals for this year is to do more with ASP.NET so [Alex](http://www.alexthissen.nl/) isn’t the only one with all the knowledge 😉

Some fun things are:
* Vegas! Need I say more?!
* A lot on ASP.NET, Ajax and WPF/E. I haven’t looked into Ajax and WPF/e much yet, so I should learn a lot here.
* I’m particularly interested in the success stories behind really large websites.
* Rumors that new stuff will be presented at MIX’07, for example still-secret “Technology X”
* I’ll stay at the Venetian, a great hotel with over 4000 rooms, 70 shops and multiple other facilities. Oh, and a casino as well 😉
* Windows Vista Ultimate and Expression Studio free for all registrants
* ASP.NET and PHP head-to-head.  
I’ll be attending this [conversation](http://www.visitmix.com/Blogs/News/its-on-asp-and-php-go-head-to-head-at-mix07/) as I’ve never looked at PHP and I’m interested on where the discussion will lead to. I’m hoping for both a religious discussion (for the fun) as a serious discussion on pros and cons of both.
* Pussycat dolls at Pure? I’m might take a look, but I’m not sure I’m really into that…

I’ll keep you informed right here. For all Dutch readers, I’ll try and post some stories at [my personal weblog](http://lekkerouderwets.nl/blogs/dennis/).
