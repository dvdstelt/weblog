Today Typemock released a new product called [Isolator for SharePoint](http://www.typemock.com/sharepointpage.php?utm_source=sp_bb&utm_medium=blog_4sp&utm_campaign=sp_bb). If you’re confused, Typemock is the company and Isolator is their mocking framework you might (and should!) know.

The new product is just for SharePoint as it can only test SharePoint related APIs. There’s some extremely cool stuff in there, on which I will definitely blog in the near future. I’m spending quite some time currently with Isolator and it’s extremely powerful. And I’m not the only one who thinks that. The Microsoft Patterns & Practices team released a SharePoint Guidance whitepaper and it advises to use Typemock Isolator as the mocking framework.

Also, if you’re one of the first 50 bloggers out there that blog a specific text on your weblog about the new product, you get a **full license for free**!!! Read more about it here on [Eli Lopian’s weblog](http://www.elilopian.com/2008/11/24/isolator-for-sharepoint-free-license/). I’ve already got mine, so no need for me to steal your license! 😉
