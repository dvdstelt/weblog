Last year a technology preview of the Microsoft Tools for Domain-Specific Languages was [released](http://www.microsoft.com/downloads/details.aspx?FamilyId=57A14CC6-C084-48DD-B401-1845013BF834&displaylang=en). A few days ago, a set of [three documents with a walkthrough](http://www.microsoft.com/downloads/thankyou.aspx?FamilyID=3b5408e7-5ae0-468a-a749-f61ecd4eba0b&displaylang=en) was added to this, to help you work with and understand the tools.

More information can be found on the special [Team System DSL Tools website](http://lab.msdn.microsoft.com/vs2005/teamsystem/workshop/dsltools/), where an [rss feed](http://blogs.msdn.com/jochens/category/7610.aspx/rss) can also be found.
