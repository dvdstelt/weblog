And now I’m continuing the linking fest! 🙂

The woman on the left is a super model named Sara. The reason Mark Miller is blogging about [Sara](http://community.devexpress.com/blogs/thinking/archive/2007/11/01/can-you-write-code-faster-than-this-spokesmodel.aspx) is that she was at [DevConnections](http://www.devconnections.com/) in Las Vegas. You could challenge her in a coding contest. Interesting was that she had never touched a PC before in her life! Six hours of training in DevExpress’ CodeRush and she can beat any developer out there, not using CodeRush! Isn’t that fantastic?! See [the video](http://www.devexpress.com/Products/NET/IDETools/CodeRush/sara.xml) for a little proof. You could also go watch [Gina](http://community.devexpress.com/blogs/thinking/archive/2007/11/01/can-a-sokesmodel-write-an-outlook-style-app-in-30-minutes.aspx) code an Outlook style app in under 30 minutes.

Jim Holmes practiced his code using ReSharper but failed to win from Sara! [Read about it here](http://frazzleddad.blogspot.com/2007/11/coding-for-speed-or-how-i-got-my-butt.html). Don’t forget to read the comments, where some ReSharper developer comes around and comments : “Still, our tool is better”. The arrogance! 🙂 DevExpress challenged him to come by and test his tool against Sara and CodeRush. Way to go DevExpress!

I think the idea to compete against a super model in a coding contest is an excellent idea and proves their product is worth taking a look. Especially since so many people think ReSharper is the only way to go.
