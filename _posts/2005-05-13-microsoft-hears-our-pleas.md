<font face="Arial" size="2">A while ago there was [a discussion](https://bloggingabout-linux.azurewebsites.net/dennis/archive/2005/03/23/2556.aspx) about the different versions of Visual Studio.NET and the fact that you could only get one version with your MSDN Enterprise subscription. Just posted on Rob Caron’s Team System blog, that every version (Developer, Test, Architect and Suite) will include a 5-user version of Team Foundation server. </font><font face="Arial" size="2">This is good news, as part of the discussion was about independent developers not getting the Team Foundation server software for free.</font>

<font face="Arial" size="2">There’s also some more info on the licensing by [Rick LaPlante](https://blogs.msdn.com/rickla/archive/2005/05/12/416994.aspx) and on the [official site](http://msdn.microsoft.com/howtobuy/vs2005/).</font>
