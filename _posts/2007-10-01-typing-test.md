It began on [Daniel Moth](http://www.danielmoth.com/Blog/2007/09/i-type-50-wpm.html)‘s weblog, reading about some typing test he had done with some other folks. Today I read about it again on [Scott Hanselman](http://feeds.feedburner.com/~r/ScottHanselman/~3/163073553/TypingTestTwiceOnceWithVoiceRecognition.aspx)‘s website and decided to take the test myself.

My results:  
Number of words typed: **240** Test duration: **3 min** Speed: **80.1 words/min**. (400 keystrokes/min.)  
Error penalty: **15** Accuracy: **93.8%** It’s quite funny to see that I score higher then lots of the native English speakers. My accuracy isn’t that high, but some Americans are around 60 words per minute.

You can [do the test yourself](http://www.typequick.com/ttest/testyourskills.html). Remember to press enter (twice) for only new paragraphs. The text will start scrolling by itself once you reach a number of characters.
