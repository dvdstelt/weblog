I’m kinda bored at work with no account to logon to the development network, so nothing to do but browse the internet.

Just browsed tweakers.net and found a tool called [DOSBox](http://dosbox.sourceforge.net/), which emulates MS-DOS. But, does more! It emulates realmode/protected mode, XMS/EMS, VESA Graphics as well as a good old Gravis UltraSound (GUS).

Together with a nice list on [pain.scene.org](http://pain.scene.org/service_dosbox.php) you can see that demos like [Dope by Complex](http://www.pouet.net/prod.php?which=37), [Headache by Psychic Link](http://www.pouet.net/prod.php?which=1150), [Second Reality by Future Cew](http://www.pouet.net/prod.php?which=63), [Solstice by Valhalla](http://www.pouet.net/prod.php?which=106), [Stars by NoooN](http://www.pouet.net/prod.php?which=301) and lots of other demos work within this emulator!

Too bad I have family coming over tonight, because I want to try these babies out!

[Get DOSBox here](http://dosbox.sourceforge.net/).
