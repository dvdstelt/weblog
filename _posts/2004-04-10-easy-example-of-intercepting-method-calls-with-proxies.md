For a few of my collegues, probably the first time they heard of intercepting methods by proxies was when the main developer of our E-Platform explained the communication BUS in this platform. I just noticed a link on [asp.net](http://www.asp.net/) which contains a very basic and very easily understandable example on how to use these proxies.

With proxies intercepting methods calls, you can transform the data transported, verify if a user is authorized to call the method and much more. You can [find the example here](http://www.kersting-it.net/howto/proxy.html).
